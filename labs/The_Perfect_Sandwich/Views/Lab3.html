Roast Pork and Pickled Cucumber Sandwich

Description

I thought this tasty and it was. Stuff your face.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless pork loin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • kosher salt and black pepper
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 4 Portuguese rolls, split

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 450° F.
  2. Season the pork with the cayenne and ¼ teaspoon salt.
  3. Roast on a rimmed baking sheet until an instant-read thermometer inserted in the center reaches 145° F, roughly 20 to 25 minutes;
  4. Let rest for 3 minutes, then thinly slice.
  5. Meanwhile, combine the cucumber, vinegar, oil, brown sugar, and ¼ teaspoon each salt and black pepper in a medium bowl.
  6. Divide the mayonnaise, pork, and cucumber amongst the rolls.
  7. Enjoy!
